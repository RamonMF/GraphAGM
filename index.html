Visualization tool for a corpus of ancient greek technical works

This tool focuses on the study of a corpus of ancient greek works in the period from 350 B.C. to 700 A.D. The tool consists of the following two graphs:

Timeline:

Represents the distribution of works in time and its classification based on various attributes (content, type, style, etc.). The graph is interactive and can be filtered and adjusted by various parameters. Please look at the help for detailed instructions about this graph or click on the image to start.

Zoomable sunburst:

Dynamic visualization of the categories, authors and works in a circular graph formed by one ring for every category, one ring for the authors and one for the works. The order of this rings can be changed dinamically. By moving the mouse over the graph a secuence of arcs is selected representing a subgroup of works. By clicking on some arc this becomes the center of the graph and its child-arcs are zoomed in allowing a more in detail view. Statistics of works/characters are showed for every selection. Please look at the help for detailed instructions about this graph or click on the image to start.
